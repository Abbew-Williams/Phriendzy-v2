# **App Name**: Vibeflow

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Profile: Users can sign up, log in via email or social accounts, and create/manage their personal profiles including picture, bio, username, and public/private status.
- Short Video & Photo Sharing: Users can upload short videos (up to 3 minutes) and photos, add simple captions, and share them on their feed.
- AI-Powered Discovery Tool: An algorithmic tool that recommends personalized content (videos and photos) to users based on their interactions, watch time, and following activity.
- Content Feed & Interaction: Display a dynamic feed of content from followed users and the discovery algorithm, allowing users to like and comment on posts.
- Following & Connection System: Users can follow and unfollow other users, building a personalized social network and viewing content from their connections.
- Real-time Direct Messaging: Basic real-time, one-on-one text messaging functionality for private communication between users.
- Content Reporting & Blocking: A mechanism for users to report inappropriate content or behavior and block other users for safety and content moderation.

## Style Guidelines:

- A vibrant and expressive dark color scheme, inspired by the dynamic nature of modern social media platforms, providing a sleek backdrop for engaging content.
- Primary interactive elements will use a bold and creative violet-purple, contrasting with the dark background. (Hex: #9951E0)
- The background will be a very dark, subtly purple-tinted grey, offering a sophisticated and contemporary feel. (Hex: #261E2E)
- An electric blue accent will highlight key actions and provide visual pop, chosen for its strong contrast and energy. (Hex: #7575EF)
- Headlines and prominent text will use 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-inspired, and slightly avant-garde aesthetic.
- Body text and smaller informational elements will utilize 'Inter' (sans-serif) for excellent readability, a clean finish, and universal applicability.
- Utilize minimalist, line-art style icons that are modern and intuitive, reinforcing the app's clean aesthetic without clutter.
- A full-screen, immersive content feed with media-centric cards that prioritize visual content. Use consistent spacing and modular components for scalability.
- Subtle, fluid transitions and micro-interactions for likes, comments, and navigation to enhance user engagement and provide a polished experience.
